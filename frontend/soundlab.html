♫ Sound lab

Audition each set, then click Use — your choice is saved in this browser and applied on the board. Run scripts/fetch_sounds.py first to download the sets.
SetAudition

Piano, NES, SFX and Futuristic are the freely-licensed Lichess sets by Enigmahack (AGPLv3+). Lichess's standard sounds are non-free, so they're not options here. "Synth" is this app's built-in Web Audio engine — zero files, always available.
